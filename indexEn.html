F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Current tender opportunities can be accessed by visiting the tender website, registering as a new user, and viewing current tender announcements. You can also subscribe to a newsletter to receive updates on new tender opportunities.

An offer may be submitted for the tender by entering the tender website and reviewing the details of the offered offer. The user must ensure that the deadline for submission of bids has passed and follow the instructions available on the site.

The criteria that your company must follow to qualify to participate in tenders vary according to the type of project and the sector under which the project falls. You can view the requirements and criteria for qualification by visiting the tender website and reviewing the general conditions for participation in tenders.

Restrictions on the number of bids that can be submitted for the same project vary between countries and regions. The conditions for the announced project can be viewed

The deadlines for submitting bids vary according to the advertised opportunity and bidding rules

Tenders get their fees by deducting a small percentage of the profits of the company that the owner chooses to implement his project.
